|Him| |"His" Theme|





A rare video found of
TMO when he is seemingly out of character
and not the energetic and active person people seem to know.

For this final project, I was inspired to go for a more anime approach since characters in anime have their own unique theme
that defines and expands upon the character. I combined two simple concepts such as the still stance of my character and the simple melody in the back
to create a unique atmosphere of my own. I would more consider this as a combination between an animation short and a music video since neither
the animation nor the music outhshines each other, they both work in parallel. The overall video is supposed to convey a true sense of peace in a mystery,
where the tone feels ominou but the beautiful moon and calming winds makes you feel more calm than anxious. Originally I had planned to add 2 scenes in which
my character clears his throat and creates a void in one hand, and fire in the other, but I thought that would have taken away from the overall mood that I wanted to convey
and so I felt the extra work would have been unnecessary. In the end, I created a loop that lasted for 1 second and I duplicated that loop enough in order to match the time limit of the song.
I gave the clip a slow fade in and fade out to ease the viewers into the atmosphere as thrusting them into it would defeat the purpose and the outcome of
the final animation came out as good as the idea that I originally had in my head.
